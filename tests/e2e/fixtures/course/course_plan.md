# Course Plan — E2E Fixture Course

A deliberately tiny plan used only by the course-maker e2e smoke tests.

## Lecture 01 — Sampling and Aliasing

**Goal:** show why sampling rate matters.

- A continuous sine wave and its samples.
- Aliasing: a high-frequency signal masquerading as a low-frequency one.
- The Nyquist rate as the takeaway.

Target: ~6 slides. Two figures (a sampled sine, an aliasing illustration).

## Lecture 02 — The Normal Distribution

**Goal:** introduce the Gaussian and the effect of its parameters.

- The bell curve shape.
- How the mean shifts it and the standard deviation widens it.

Target: ~5 slides. One or two figures (PDF shapes for varying parameters).

## Quiz 01 — Sampling basics

Short check on lecture 01: a couple of single-choice questions and one
short-answer item on the Nyquist rate.
